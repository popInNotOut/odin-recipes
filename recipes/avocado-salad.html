Description

This avocado salad is a delicious combination of creamy avocados, sweet and juicy tomatoes, bell pepper, cilantro, and fresh lime juice for an easy summer salad full of bold, fresh flavor and color


Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Seasonings (Salt and pepper)

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine avocados, onion, bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, and lime juice in a large bowl
  3. Gently toss until evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Enjoy!
