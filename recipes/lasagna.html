Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves you lots of time.


Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses (cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings (dried parsley, salt, and black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef
  2. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  3. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
  4. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  5. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  6. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
